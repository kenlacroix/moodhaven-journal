Your journal holds powerful stories about your growth. MoodHaven Insights lets you explore them — without giving up your privacy.

## The Core Principle

Every insight MoodHaven generates is computed locally, on your device, after decrypting your entries in memory. Nothing is sent out. No cloud processing, no third-party models, no data leaving your machine.

Your words stay yours. Always.

## What's Already in MoodHaven

The current release includes several built-in analytics features:

- **Mood trends over time** — track how your mood shifts across days, weeks, and months
- **Day-of-week patterns** — see which days you tend to feel best (and worst)
- **Journaling streaks** — your current and longest streak, total days journaled
- **Mood distribution** — a breakdown of how often you hit each mood level
- **Tag analysis** — see which themes appear most in your writing

All of this runs against your local encrypted database. The app decrypts, computes, displays — and never writes the results anywhere.

## AI-Powered Insights (Coming)

We're adding a layer of AI insights that respects the same rules:

- **Local-first:** analyses use locally extracted metadata (mood scores, sentiment, writing patterns) — never raw journal text
- **Opt-in:** disabled by default, requires explicit setup
- **BYOK option:** bring your own OpenAI key, or run everything through a local Ollama instance
- **Consent-gated:** the UI walks you through exactly what data is used before enabling anything

The goal is to help you notice patterns you might miss — not to hand your private writing to a cloud service.

## Writing Prompts

MoodHaven already generates contextual writing prompts based on your recent mood patterns and journaling frequency. These run entirely locally — no API call required.

If your mood has been declining, you might get a prompt about what's weighing on you. If you've been on a streak, you might get a reflection on what's been working.

The prompts adapt to you, privately.

## Why Build Insights This Way?

Because your reflections are sacred spaces — not data points for someone else's analytics server.

MoodHaven isn't just about encryption — it's about respect. You should be able to learn and grow from your journal without sacrificing ownership or privacy.

Insights can empower you. But only if they respect you first.
