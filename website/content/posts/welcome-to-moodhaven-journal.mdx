Thank you for being here.

MoodHaven Journal started from a simple but powerful need — the need for a quieter space.

- A place to reflect.
- A place to grow.
- A place to breathe again in a world that often feels overwhelming.

MoodHaven Journal is our way of creating that space — a digital haven where your thoughts are safe, your story is honored, and your growth is truly your own.

## What We're Building

Over the coming months, we're sharing:

- Early updates as we build and refine MoodHaven Journal
- Reflections on mindfulness, journaling habits, and emotional resilience
- Behind-the-scenes glimpses into designing a calmer, more intentional app
- Invitations for early feedback, beta testing, and community growth

Our hope is simple: to create something that feels less like an app... and more like a deep breath.

## Privacy First, Always

From day one, MoodHaven is built around one belief: your thoughts belong to you — not to us, not to anyone else.

Every journal entry is encrypted on your device. Nothing is uploaded without your explicit choice. No account required to start writing.

We're building this in public, on [GitHub](https://github.com/kenlacroix/moodhaven-journal), so you can see every line of code and every decision we make.

If this journey resonates with you, follow along and share MoodHaven Journal with someone who might appreciate a little more calm in their day.

We're excited to build this with you.

— The MoodHaven Journal Team
